ELIGIBILITY TO JOIN DIPLOMA COURSES:


1. The candidates who have passed S.S.C or those who are appearing for S.S.C.should write Common Entrance Examination for Polytechnic.The applications for the POLYCET can be had from any polytechnic in the state.
2. The Govt. will fill the seats into the diploma courses through the online method,according to the ranks obtained by the candidates in POLYCET.Admission schedule will be given in daily newspapers. The candidates can take admission at the admission camp by paying required fee.
3. After completion of the admission process if the seats are vacant the management of the polytechnic can fill giving priorty first to the POLYCET rank holders and next to the other candidates i.e., S.S.C. passed candidate.
4. For any information regarding POLYCET & admissions contact cell No.9966686659, 8686748333 and see in web https://polycetts.nic.in/
