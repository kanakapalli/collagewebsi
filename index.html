About Us

VMR Polytechnic (VMR) is Sponsored by Manohar Educational Society, which was established in 1994. The polytechnic is situated 7 Kms away from Kazipet Railway Station at Hanumakoda - Hyderabad National Highway, Telagana, India. VMR polytechnic is located in 10.375 acres of land with lush green and pollution free environment. The polytechnic has nearly 1 lakh sqft built up area for teaching and laboratory purpose. All Labs are well equipped.
